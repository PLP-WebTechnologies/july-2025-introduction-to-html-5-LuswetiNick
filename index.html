About Our Community Garden

Welcome to Green Valley Community Garden, where neighbors come together to cultivate not just plants, but lasting friendships and sustainable practices. Since 2018, our garden has been a thriving hub for organic gardening, environmental education, and community connection.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of community-driven agriculture to strengthen neighborhoods, promote healthy eating, and protect our environment. Our mission is to provide accessible garden space, education, and resources to help everyone grow their own fresh, organic produce.

Our Programs

Plot Rental Program

Rent your own 4x8 foot garden plot for the growing season. Perfect for beginners and experienced gardeners alike. Includes access to tools, water, and composting facilities.

  • Individual plots: KES 50000 per season
  • Family plots: KES 7500000 per season
  • Senior discount: 25% off

Educational Workshops

Learn sustainable gardening techniques through our monthly workshops led by master gardeners and local experts.

  • Organic pest management
  • Composting basics
  • Seed starting techniques
  • Seasonal garden planning

Youth Garden Program

Engaging children ages 6-16 in hands-on gardening experiences that teach responsibility, nutrition, and environmental stewardship.
